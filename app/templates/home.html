Know about our Trans Asian Chamber of Commerce & industry

Empowering Your Vision, Fueling Your Growth

The Trans Asian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (TACCI), was founded in 1999 with the express purpose of promoting best business practices, building mutual respect and trust among the trade community for the good of the society, the country, and the entire world. TACCI offers a forum for networking and reaching agreements between industries. It is a non-governmental, non-profit organization that firmly adheres to

Vasudhaiva Kutumbakaam ॥ वसुधैव कुटुंबकम् ॥ 'All people are members of one global family'
