About TACCI

Trans Asian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (TACCI), a non-governmental, non-profit organization is dedicated to promoting fair business practices between developed and developing nations. TACCI offers business promotions and global opportunities across various countries. With authentic connections spanning 70 countries and a membership exceeding 1,200, particularly in Hindustaan and numerous other nations, TACCI has led delegations and participated actively in events worldwide, including Ethiopia, Namibia, Egypt, Iran, Palestine, Nepal, China, Thailand, Hong Kong, Kazakhstan, South Korea, Spain, Poland, and the USA. At its core, TACCI serves as a dynamic networking platform which brings together diplomats, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders from Asia and beyond, fostering meaningful connections. Through regular meetings, seminars, and networking events, the chamber cultivates an environment conducive to establishing enduring business relationships.
