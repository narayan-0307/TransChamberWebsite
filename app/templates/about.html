About Us

Trans Asian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (TACCI), a non-governmental, non-profit organization is dedicated to promoting fair business practices between developed and developing nations. TACCI offers business promotions and global opportunities across various countries. With authentic connections spanning 70 countries and a membership exceeding 1,200, particularly in Hindustaan and numerous other nations, TACCI has led delegations and participated actively in events worldwide, including Ethiopia, Namibia, Egypt, Iran, Palestine, Nepal, China, Thailand, Hong Kong, Kazakhstan, South Korea, Spain, Poland, and the USA. At its core, TACCI serves as a dynamic networking platform which brings together diplomats, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders from Asia and beyond, fostering meaningful connections. Through regular meetings, seminars, and networking events, the chamber cultivates an environment conducive to establishing enduring business relationships.

Ten Commandments or Dashaadnaa of TACCI

  • 1
    To promote exports and Imports from Hindustan, all over the world particularly in new markets.
  • 2
    To encourage Hindustani's to travel to new countries to seek new markets.
  • 3
    To monitor fair business practice in business transactions between developed and developing countries to avoid unethical exploitation in the financial transactions.
  • 4
    To take up environmental issues in developing countries with pragmatic approach.
  • 5
    To promote awareness towards ecology and environment among professionals, Industrialists in developing countries with active support of developed countries.
  • 6
    To promote quality conscious attitude among manufacturers and industrialists in developing countries.
  • 7
    To promote work-culture among manufacturers and industrialists in developing countries.
  • 8
    To acknowledge the talents of the people related to Research and Development in Eco - related sciences, Industrial Growth, Public welfare social cause offering them proper support and platform.
  • 9
    To promote Trans Asian Bank for development projects in developing countries under the guidelines of World Bank & IMF.
  • 10
    To promote population control in Hindustan and other developing countries with other health priorities.
